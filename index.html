Energy

Matcha Botanicals' matcha tea contains 100 times more epigallocatechin (an antioxidant more powerful than vitamins C and E) than any other tea, as matcha tea leaves are harvested in the first ten days of May and June and contain a high concentration of chlorophyll.

Alternative to coffee

A typical serving of matcha has the same amount of caffeine as a cup of coffee. The only difference is that the local caffeine forms a special compound with tannins - thein. This alkaloid is excellent for invigorating, but acts gently, without pressure spikes.
